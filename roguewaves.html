Rogue Waves: A Pirate Adventure

For the best experience, play in fullscreen mode.

Instructions

  • Shooting Phase: Hold the left mouse button, drag back, and release to fire. Adjust power and angle with the mouse.
  • Movement Phase: Use A/D or arrow keys to move your ship horizontally. You have 3 seconds to reposition.
  • Power Ups: Activate power-ups by clicking the crewmate icon. Cooldowns apply between uses.

Controls:

  • Hold and drag the left mouse button to shoot.
  • Use A/D or arrow keys to move.
